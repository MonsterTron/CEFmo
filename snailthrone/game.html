Welcome to the Great Project of FOMO!


WHAT IS CRYPTO FOMO?

Crypto FOMO is a King of the Hill game: your goal is to climb to the top of the pyramid, and stay there long enough without anyone booting you off the throne.

To claim the throne, you must sacrifice FOMO. FOMO can be bought, hatched, and sold back to the game itself.

In practical terms, CRYPTO FOMO is a smart contract running on the Bsc blockchain. You must have MetaMask or a compatible trust wallet to play.

MORE INFO ON METAMASK


WHAT IS BSC?

BSC is a decentralized network where programs can run autonomously. Crypto Fomo is one such program. Nobody owns the game, and thousands of computers all over the world execute the game's programming as part of the BSC network.

You may also know BNB as a cryptocurrency. The currency of the BSC network is called BNB. BNB is used to pay for transactions, as well as exchanges of value.

CRYPTO FOMO requires BNB for both transactions and exchanges of value.


HOW DOES CRYPTO FOMO?

CRYPTO FOMO is a deterministic game. There is no randomness to the rules. Your actions have fixed outcomes, and the same goes for every player.

To start, you will need to buy FOMO. Once you own a few FOMO, a variety of actions is available to you.

You can sacrifice some of your FOMO to claim the throne as the Pharaoh. If no other player is willing to sacrifice enough FOMO until the timer is over, you win this round's jackpot.

Your FOMO lay MOJO consistently. You can hatch these MOJO into even more FOMO, or you can feed these MOJO to the FrogKing to get some ETH back.

At any point in the game, you can sell your FOMO back to the game. The BNB you will get for this action may be higher or lower than the ETH you spent to buy your FOMO, based on several factors expanded below.

Last but not least, holding FOMO entitles you to a share of the BNB spent on buying or hatching FOMO.

As long as you have FOMO, you will passively get BNB for as long as other players participate.

IS THE GAME SECURE?

Smart contracts act exactly as defined, and the source code is open for anyone to read.

The game lives on the BSC blockchain and would go on without interruption even if the website went down.

The bsc blockchain itself is secured by hundreds of millions of dollars worth of redundancy. Your bank is less secure than this!

The biggest risks with decentralized applications have to do with *your own* security.

Transactions are irreversible, and nobody can refund you if you're not happy with your purchase.

Make sure you write your private key down. Keep it safe, and don't share it with anyone else.

IS THIS AN INVESTMENT VEHICLE?

No! This is a zero-sum game, meaning each participant's gains are balanced by another participant's losses.

CRYPTO FOMO is a game of math and logic, with no randomness involved. The correct strategy will lead to success, and there can be many winners. But there will be many losers as well.

Even though CRYPTO FOMO isn't gambling, you shouldn't play this game if you're susceptible to gambling addiction.

Make sure you familiarize yourself with the rules, and don't play with BNB you can't afford to lose. If you want to try out the game in a safe environment, head over to the TEST version on the Ropsten network, where BNB is free.

QUICK START


BUY and SELL FOMO (pyramid tokens) directly from the contract.

FOMO holders receive proportional DIVIDENDS from buys and hatches, as well as from other Snail games (in the future).

Token price correlates with FOMO max supply. More snails = higher price.

FOMO produce MOJO, at a rate of 8% per day. A hatchery can hold as many MOJOs as there are snails.

Players can HATCH these MOJOs to turn them into more FOMO. Hatching still costs BNB, but only half of the normal price.

Players can also FEED their MOJOs to the FrogKing for an BNB reward.

Players can SACRIFICE a certain amount of FOMO to hold the title of Pharaoh. The Pharaoh earns dividends on every buy, and if the timer runs out, the Pharaoh can claim the GodPot.

NOTE: buying more FOMO will reset your MOJOs to 0, and selling or making a sacrifice will reduce your number of MOJOs. Make sure to hatch or feed before any other action if you have a significant number of MOJOs!


BNB distribution on buy:

- 50% saved for the FOMOPot (token price on sale)

- 20% distributed in divs

- 20% go to the FrogPot

- 2% is given to the current Pharaoh

- 2% goes to the GodPot

- 6% goes to the referral. If there is no referral, it goes to the FOMOGod pot


BNB distribution on hatch:

- 40% in divs

- 40% to the FrogPot

- 4% to the current Pharaoh

- 16% to the GodPot


FOMOPOT (sell fund)

WARNING: selling FOMO will make you lose a proportional amount of MOJOs. Hatch them or feed the FrogKing before a sacrifice!

FOMO can be sold to the FOMOPot for BNB.

Price per FOMO is 50% of the current buy price.

No more than 10% of the FOMOPot can be drained in one sale!

This means it is easier to sell small quantities of FOMO than large ones.

Regardless, a player can chain sales one after another, without downtime.


FOMOPOT (feed MOJO fund)

Feeding MOJO to the frogking grants a reward.

BNB earned = FOMOpot * MOJO fed / total FOMO


GODPOT (jackpot fund)

Given to the current Pharaoh when the timer hits 0. This timer starts at 24 hours on each new round.

Sacrifice the indicated FOMO amount to become the Pharaoh. While the Pharaoh sits on the throne, he receives BNB from every buy and hatch.

Every 12 seconds, one FOMO is removed from the required sacrifice. The minimum required is 40 FOMOs.

A successful sacrifice will bump the timer back up by 8 minutes, and add the amount of FOMO sacrificed to the requirement.

Once the timer hits 0, whoever holds the Pharaoh title ascends to godhood. This new FOMOGod can claim the round pot.

After the pot is claimed, the timer resets at 24 hours. Whoever starts the new round is Pharaoh.

PLEASE NOTE: sacrificing FOMO will make you lose a proportional amount of MOJOS. Hatch them or feed the FrogKing before a sacrifice!


REFERRALS

Addresses aren't bound to their referral link. Referrals don't profit from hatching MOJOs.
